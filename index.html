Process

How It Works

We take proven open-source base models and fine-tune them on your domain data using our training pipeline.

STEP 01

Data Audit

We review your domain data, identify reasoning tasks, and define the benchmark we'll use to measure success. You know what good answers look like — we structure the training around that.

STEP 02

Fine-Tuning

We fine-tune a base model (Qwen, Llama, Mistral) using LoRA — efficient, targeted training. Delivered as adapter weights or a hosted endpoint.

STEP 03

Benchmark & Deliver

We run your model against a defined benchmark before delivery. You get a score, not just a model. Hosted API or weights — your choice.
